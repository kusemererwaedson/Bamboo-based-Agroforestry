Through both our proprietary sourcing technology and strategic partnerships, our investment committee reviews a mix of on-market and off-market opportunities across the United States. Roughly 2% of deals that enter our pipeline are eventually offered on our platform.

We are opening a Rolling Fund to invest in both of these opportunities alongside us. Most of the fund will be used for traditional angel checks but we may use a portion of it to do secondaries and other oddities that come uniquely to us due to our reputation and nature as a holding company and venture builder.
As always, we’re not in the business of gathering fees with no skin in the game so we are committed to charging no ongoing management fees while also being the largest single investor in the fund. You are investing with us and we will only make money when you do.

What we do

Hi, we’re TIGPE Investments. You might know us because we’ve been setting up wonderful agribusinesses for over two years. Typically, we collaborate with local land owners and set up agribusinesses in partnership. Founders partner with us because we make the partnership a breezy experience, keep things simple and straightforward, and then give the businesses what they need to be profitable and sustainable over the long term. To date, we have 30+ businesses ranging from small startups, producer cooperatives to an industrial park and processing companies, together they have over 60 employees and Ugx1,000M+ in annual revenue.
