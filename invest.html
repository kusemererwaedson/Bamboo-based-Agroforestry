Ethco = Ethical + Collaborative

Through the TIGPE Investment platform, Ethco, we raise funds from investors to invest alongside us in local businesses.


We didn't set out to become investors.

We started our business in 2014 to provide quality planting materials to farmers, and before we knew it, we had hundreds of farmers needing financial support. Along the way, we approached financial institutions to lend a hand, but it was always a miserable experience.


We asked ourselves why financial institutions dragged out deals for months and renegotiated over and over again and still not deliver. It was horrible. Then we read about Musharakah that facilitates billion-dollar opportunities quickly by keeping the process painless and straight forward. Nobody had done this for smallholder farmers like us, so we decided to do it ourselves.


In 2020, we incorporated TIGPE and started collaborating with farmers as Partner, Shareholder or Leaser using a simple one-month contracting process, then leaving the farmers alone to do their thing while we oversee.

It turned out we were right, farmers loved it.

Here's How we do it

1. Farmer enrollment

We organize meetings with the help of area LC1 and record details of the farmers in attendance: (the location, sub location, farmer name, the ID number of the farmers, thumb print, copy of ID if available, area of the farmers’ farms, area a farmer is willing to plant). Individual farm checks by the our agroforestry agents are done to evaluate the suitability of the farmer to join the program. If eligible, the farmer is formally contracted

2. Input Distribution and Planting

TIGPE Nurseries, JV sell to farmers the seedlings, or give as its share of the Musharakah. We provide other services – land opening, etc. In the Musharakah arrangement, if farmers don’t grow, neither of us gets paid. Farmers do have to set aside land plus growing the crops. Our Field Extension Network teams provide on-farm training and support.

3. Plantation Maintenance

Our Field Extension Network teams provide on-farm training and support. They collect data and monitor the plantations – and everything is coordinated through our tech platform. Most of our field staff are farmers themselves, and are our most important brand ambassadors

4. Sustainable Harvesting

Our field team and remote management technology regularly monitors and measures the progress, so we know when crops are ready for harvesting. Our specialized team harvests, then we ship it to our centralized processing centre (CPC) for processing
