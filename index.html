About Us

TIGPE builds and manages agriculture projects and enabling infrastructure and services through investment, propagation and plantation, and processing.Our investment platform, Ethifin, is approved and regulated to serve ordinary people, high-net-worth individuals, corporates and institutions. Ethifin, regulated by Uganda Capital Market Authority, is an Ethical Private Capital Marketplace platform that focuses on sustainable and impact-driven crowd-investments and financing. We are best known for our impact investment Rolling Fund which has been operational since 2022. Ethifin offers access to investment opportunities in distinctive and promising businesses while also serving as a platform for entrepreneurs to showcase their ventures to a community of global investors.

Propagation

TIGPE propagation and plantation operations is set up to produce tissue culture plants of different species of plants covering Bamboo, Horticultural plants, Forestry trees, Herbal Plants, Ornamental Plants and Agricultural Plants that are free from diseases. We support farmers through extension services to enable best agronomic practices for improved production

Plantation

Our processing operation envisages to develop state-of-the-Art infrastructure & technology in the agro-processing industry and facilitate end to end linkage, from the farmer to the consumer.
