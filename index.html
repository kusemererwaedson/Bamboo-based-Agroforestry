About Us

TIGPE builds and manages agriculture projects and enabling infrastructures and services through investment, propagation, plantation and processing with a focus on Bamboo-based agroforestry

Investment

Our investment platform, We are best known for our impact investment Rolling Fund which has been operational since 2022. Ethifin offers access to investment opportunities in distinctive and promising businesses while also serving as a platform for entrepreneurs to showcase their ventures to a community of global investors.

Read More

Propagation

TIGPE propagation and plantation operations is set up to produce tissue culture plants of different species of plants covering Bamboo, Horticultural plants, Forestry trees, Herbal Plants, Ornamental Plants and Agricultural Plants that are free from diseases. We support farmers through extension services to enable best agronomic practices for improved production

Read More

Plantation

We enroll farmers; support them through land preparation; distribute inputs and seedlings; plant with them; ensure post- planting care and long-term maintenance

Read More

Processing

Our processing operation envisages to develop state-of-the-Art infrastructure & technology in the agro-processing industry and facilitate end to end linkage, from the farmer to the consumer. .
